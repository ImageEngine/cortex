Detailed Description

The UniformRandomPointDistributionOp distributes points over a mesh using a random distribution. Evenness is approximated by weighting the amount of expected particles per mesh face to be proportional to that face's area. For a fast, even distribution, the PointDistributionOp may be preferable to this one. However, if the mesh UVs are poorly layed out, this op may be the best choice.

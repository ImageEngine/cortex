Detailed Description

The MotionPrimitive is a simple means of constructing and rendering a motionBegin/motionEnd block containing a Primitive. There's nothing to stop you from calling motionBegin/motionEnd to construct motion blocks yourself but this form is simple, and loadable and savable and copyable and all that.
